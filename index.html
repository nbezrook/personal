in text media CV

Niel Bezrookove. I was born in the Netherlands. I am a writer & new media artist. I graduated from the University of California, San Diego in 2018 with a degree in Cognitive Science (B.S.). I was a resident at the Culture, Art, Technology department of the University of California, San Diego and had my work appear in the Museum of Contemporary Art, San Diego. My research interests include the economics of technology, postmodern aesthetics, evolutionary psychology, philosophy of mathematics, and ontology in the Internet age. I've lectured on philosophy, cognitive science, and systems theory.

My creative work is in contemporary art & avant-garde music. I've been part of several performance groups including La Montagne, Casi Casi, and Bill's Acountant. My own projects include Smogcat, Tomo Takino, and The Wretched Excess.

Currently looking to publish "The Pupa Woman," a contemporary work of fiction concerning technology and desire. Also in the early stages of writing another long-form work, tentatively named "Thirsting for Annihilation."
